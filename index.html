About us

Outstanding laundry service

Fast Laundry is committed to delivering best-in-class laundry services in Dubai, with innovation, excellence, and attention. Since 2010, our brand has gained popularity in Al Warqa, Al Qusais, Nad Al Hammar, Mirdiff, and Arjan as a trusted laundry partner for delivering timely, hassle-free laundry services. We offer ironing, dry cleaning, special clothing care, and washing services, all customized to fit our customers’ needs.

Over the years, we've expanded to cater to a wide audience that now recognizes our pioneering efforts and flawless on-time delivery, green practices, and attention to detail. At Fast Laundry, customer satisfaction is our priority, and we ensure that every garment receives the utmost care.
